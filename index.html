Steps to Make a Great Cup of Coffee

  1. Start with fresh, high-quality coffee beans.
  2. Grind the beans just before brewing to a consistency appropriate for your brewing method.
  3. Heat fresh, filtered water to the optimal temperature (around 90-96°C or 195-205°F).
  4. Choose your preferred brewing method (e.g., French press, pour-over, espresso).
  5. Carefully measure your coffee-to-water ratio.
  6. Pour the hot water over the grounds evenly.
  7. Allow the coffee to steep for the recommended time.
    1. French Press: 4 minutes
    2. Pour Over: 2-3 minutes
    3. Espresso: 25-30 seconds
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Image with Text Wrap

This paragraph demonstrates how you can wrap text around an image. By using the `float` CSS property, the image can be moved to one side, and the text will flow around it. This is a common layout technique for articles and blog posts where you want the image to be integrated more tightly with the content, rather than simply sitting above or below it. It helps in creating a more visually engaging and compact layout for your content.
